Radiological Society of Mauritius and Jaipur Institute of Pain & Sports Injuries (JIPSI)

Highlights of the course :
– Hands-on Musculoskeletal Ultrasound Workshop (7:1 Faculty to delegates ratio) 
– Practical reporting pearls on Musculoskeletal MRI with interactive lectures 
– Mix of onsite and online Interactive sessions by Experts in various subspecialties within Musculoskeletal Medicine
– Special focus on Image guided Musculoskeletal Interventions with an introductory Interventions workshop and live demo on PRP Preparation. 

40% of course content aimed at beginners , 35% for Intermediate level and 25% content for advanced level 

Total 20 Hours of Interactive lectures on Musculoskeletal Ultrasound, sports medicine, Pain management , Rheumatology, MSK Interventions
