COURSE HOSTED BY:

Radiological Society of Mauritius and Jaipur Institute of Pain & Sports Injuries (JIPSI)

Highlights of the course :
– Hands-on Musculoskeletal Ultrasound Workshop (7:1 Faculty to delegates ratio) 
– Practical reporting pearls on Musculoskeletal MRI with interactive lectures 
– Mix of onsite and online Interactive sessions by Experts in various subspecialties within Musculoskeletal Medicine
– Special focus on Image guided Musculoskeletal Interventions with an introductory Interventions workshop and live demo on PRP Preparation. 

40% of course content aimed at beginners , 35% for Intermediate level and 25% content for advanced level 

Total 20 Hours of Interactive lectures on Musculoskeletal Ultrasound, sports medicine, Pain management , Rheumatology, MSK Interventions

WELCOME MESSAGE

Dear Friends/colleagues,

On behalf of the organizing team, it is our sheer pleasure to welcome you to International Musculoskeletal Summit(IMS) to be held in Mauritius, the sun-kissed Island in the Indian Ocean. As Mark Twain famously said, ”Mauritius was made first, then Heaven”. The course is aimed at beginners while also covering intermediate level and some advanced topics in Musculoskeletal Radiology/Medicine. The delegates will be welcomed with a meet and greet event on Friday evening and beach side gala dinner accompanied with cultural events on Saturday night. The conference Venue is Long beach Hotel, a luxury 5 star beach resort in eastern part of Mauritius


MUSCULOSKELETAL ULTRASOUND WORKSHOP
Workshop aimed at delegates with Beginners to intermediate level exposure to Musculoskeletal Ultrasound with 20 percent content for Advanced level.
  • Introduction to Musculoskeletal Ultrasound – Basic theory and Knobology
  • 7:1 Delegates: Faculty ratio during the hands-on sessions
  • Lectures on anatomy and pathology before Individual joint hands-on sessions
  • High-end Ultrasound machines with linear probes available during the entire course for practice of delegates

MUSCULOSKELETAL MRI INTERACTIVE LECTURES
Global Musculoskeletal MRI Experts Like Dr Donald Resnick , Dr Mark Davies, Dr Girish Gandhikota, Dr Rajesh Botchu, Dr Varaprasad and many more will be delivering interactive Lectures on Musculoskeletal MRI
  • Technical considerations for Optimizing MSK MRI
  • MRI in Degenerative Joint diseases
  • MRI spine including seronegative Spondyloarthropathies
  • MRI in Sports medicine/Sports Injuries

MUSCULOSKELETAL INTERVENTIONS
Basic to intermediate Introduction to the science of MSK Interventions and a special live demo preparation of Platelet Rich Plasma (PRP)
  • Basics of Ultrasound Guided MSK Interventions
  • Workshop on Platelet Rich Plasma (PRP) Preparation
  • Interactive Lectures on various MSK Interventions
